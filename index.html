Some common questions were often asked

My flowers are falling off or dying?
This could be due to overwatering, underwatering, or lack of sunlight. Make sure to check the plant's specific care needs.
What causes leaves to become pale?
Pale leaves often indicate a lack of nutrients or insufficient light. Try fertilizing and providing more sunlight.
What causes brown crispy leaves?
Brown, crispy leaves can be a sign of low humidity or underwatering. Increase humidity or water the plant more frequently.
How do I choose a plant?
Plants are an easy way to add color, energy, and transform your space, but choosing the right plant is key. Consider light, size, and maintenance requirements.
How do I change the pots?
Choose a pot slightly larger than the current one, and gently transfer the plant, keeping the roots intact.
Why are gnats flying around my plant?
Gnats are often attracted to moist soil. Allow the top inch of soil to dry out between waterings to deter them.
